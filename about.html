About Beaver Printing

Printing 2D things on paper is something that is widely accessible to most people, without much hassle. We’d like to make 3D printing just as accessible! 3D printing shouldn’t just be for those who have the funds to afford a 3D printer, or the technical skills to make one themselves.

3D printing is for everybody, regardless of class or skill level.

Here at Beaver Printing and Prototyping, we treat you like family and keep you involved in every step of the process. With our Busy Beaver Guarantee™, if you aren’t completely satisfied with your product, we will send you a new one or refund your money immediately!

All these things are combined to make sure that you are completely satisfied with your product and that you feel that you have an active role in its creation!
